The George Peabody Library is located in Baltimore, Maryland. The library was completed in 1878 by Architect Edmund G Lind with Nathaniel H. Morison .


Interior of the George Peabody from the third floor stacks


The library collection comprises over 300,000 volumes and is particularly strong in religion, British art, architecture, topography and history, American history, biography and history, and literature; history of science; and geography, exploration and travel. The library interior is one of the most beautiful in the world.
